There is questions list at the first stage of the project:

1) Which criteria are engaged during the decision-making process for choosing
the best supplier?
2) How much is each criterion's priority?
3) Which parameters are involved in filtering the sensitive goods?
4) Are there any restrictions on supplier selection (based on suppliers' policies)?
5) Which supplier is preferred when several suppliers offer the same output?
6) Are there minimum and maximum values of the parameters needed for
supplier selection?
7) What should be done if the cost of the supplier service is higher than the amount
of money a customer pays?
8) Is there a possibility to increase the importance of a specific parameter for a
client?
9) What important things should be considered in the early stages of the project
to smoothly shift to plans other than “Express” in the future?
